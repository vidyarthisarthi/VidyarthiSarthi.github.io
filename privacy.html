Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21 June 2026 · Last Updated: 21 June 2026

Vidyarthi Sarthi Private Limited ("we", "us", "our") operates VidyarthiSarthi.ai, an AI-powered tutoring application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have, in accordance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP Act").

By using VidyarthiSarthi.ai, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you are under 18 years of age, this app should be used with the knowledge and consent of a parent or guardian, as required under the DPDP Act for processing children's personal data.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryWhat we collectWhy
Account detailsName, mobile number or email, board, class, preferred languageTo personalise lessons and roadmap

Voice inputSpoken answers during AI tutor sessionsConverted to text on-device or via secure processing to check understanding — see Section 3

We do not use your IP address for any account blocking, rate-limiting, or security decisions. This is a deliberate design choice to avoid wrongly affecting other users on shared mobile networks (such as Jio's CGNAT infrastructure), which is common across India.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver personalised, offline-first Physics tutoring content
  • To verify your understanding of concepts via voice or typed checkpoints
  • To manage your subscription and process renewals via Razorpay
  • To send service-related notifications (e.g., daily lesson reminders)
  • To improve our content and product based on aggregated, anonymised usage patterns

3. How Voice Data Is Handled

When you speak an answer during a lesson, your voice is converted to text using your device's built-in speech recognition wherever possible, without leaving your device. The resulting text (not the audio) may be sent to our servers to check whether your answer matches the expected concept.

For AI-generated audio responses (the tutor's voice), we use ElevenLabs to synthesise speech. We do not send your personal voice recordings to ElevenLabs — only the tutor's own scripted responses are generated through this service.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All servers are hosted in India (Mumbai region) wherever technically possible
  • Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access to production data is restricted to authorised personnel only
  • A significant portion of your lesson content is stored locally on your device to enable offline learning, and is not transmitted unless needed for sync

6. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act, 2023

As a Data Principal, you have the right to:

  • Access a summary of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erase your data and delete your account (see Section 7)
  • Withdraw consent at any time, which may limit app functionality
  • Nominate another individual to exercise these rights on your behalf in case of death or incapacity
  • Grievance redressal — raise concerns directly with us (contact below)

7. Account Deletion

You can request full deletion of your account and associated personal data directly within the app (Settings → Delete My Account), or by emailing us at vidyarthisarthi.ai@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process deletion requests within 30 days, except where we are required to retain certain records for legal or regulatory compliance (such as payment records, per Indian tax law).

8. Children's Data

VidyarthiSarthi.ai is designed for K-12 and NEET/IIT aspirant students, many of whom are minors. In accordance with the DPDP Act's provisions on children's data, we do not use your data for behavioural monitoring, targeted advertising, or tracking that could cause harm to a child. Where a user is identified as a minor, parental or guardian awareness is encouraged during sign-up.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified within the app. Continued use of VidyarthiSarthi.ai after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
